Company profile

We are pleased to convey you a brief synopsis about our company named CAREER AUMENTER. Career Aumenter is a job placement consulting firm based in Indore , M.P, India We are a young consulting firm having passion to deliver our promises as a recruitment partner and we are sure this will reflect in our services. Our aim is to provide the best job opportunities to candidates, finding the best manpower for clients. Within a short span of time. We are committed for a long term and mutually beneficial association with our clients.

Our Key Strength

  • Quality Process.
  • Transparency in the recruitment process.
  • Ethical way of doing business.
  • Having an atmosphere of trust.
  • Technology and Infrastructure.

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to become the most promising and known for quality Manpower consulting firm.
  • Desire to excel service attitude, an open culture, realisation of importance of quality.

Our Mision

  • To ensure productivity of employees and maintain harmony between the Company and its Employees.
  • To assist candidates to reach the right career.
  • Our mission is to provide comprehensive recruitment solutions to our clients and complete guidance to the candidates.

We Promise

  • To work in close coordination with our clients.
  • To provide value-added expertise during and after every assignment.
  • To execute each and every assignment diligently and with integrity.
  • To adhere to the strict norms of the client's work culture their code of ethics.
  • To thoroughly check short-listed candidates' diligence.
